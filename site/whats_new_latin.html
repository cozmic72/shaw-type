Shaw type is a typing practice tool for the Shavian alphabet, an alternative way of spelling the English language.

What's New

  • Use any keyboard you like: the app will now automatically translate QWERTY input to the keyboard layout of your choice! (Of course you can also still use your own shavian keyboard.)
  • Virtual keyboard support - see your keyboard layout on screen!
  • Improved handling of automatic ligatures: verified in Safari and Chrome - please let me know how you get on with it in your browser of choice!.
