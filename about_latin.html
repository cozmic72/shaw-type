Shaw Type is a typing practice tool designed to help you learn and master typing the Shavian alphabet on your favorite keyboard layout.

I looked around for a tool for practicing typing Shavian. I didn't find one, so I sat down to do some vibe-coding with the amazing Claude.code, and in a matter of a couple of hours, he produced the web app you see before you. We're living in the future, and we're all doomed.

Features

  • Two Practice Modes: Play mode for progressive difficulty, and Practice mode for structured lessons
  • Multiple Keyboard Layouts: Support for Shaw Imperial, Shaw QWERTY, Shaw 2-layer, and Shaw-JAFL
