Shaw Type is a typing practice tool designed to help you learn and master typing the Shavian alphabet on your favorite keyboard layout.

The Shavian alphabet is a phonetic alphabet for English designed by Ronald Kingsley Read and named after Irish playwright George Bernard Shaw, who funded a competition to design a new English alphabet.

Features

  • Two Practice Modes: Play mode for progressive difficulty, and Practice mode for structured lessons
  • Multiple Keyboard Layouts: Support for Shaw Imperial, Shaw QWERTY, Shaw 2-layer, and Shaw-JAFL

About the Project

Shaw Type was created to make learning the Shavian alphabet more accessible and enjoyable. Whether you're a beginner or looking to improve your typing speed, this tool adapts to your skill level.
