## Practice Modes

### Learn Mode
- Progressive lessons based on keyboard layout
- 10 words per level before advancing
- Layout-specific optimizations
- Real-time level information display

### Play Mode
- Progressive difficulty based on word length
- Starts with 1-2 character words
- Gradually increases to 5-7 character words
- Focuses on frequency-based vocabulary

### Practice Mode
- Custom word length selection
- Choose specific character ranges
- Practice targeted difficulty levels
- Flexible for focused improvement

## Design Philosophy

Each layout's lesson plan is designed with specific pedagogical goals:

**Shaw Imperial:** Traditional progression focusing on ergonomic finger positions
**Shaw QWERTY:** Leverages existing QWERTY muscle memory with gradual shift introduction
**Shaw 2-layer:** Phoneme-first approach, celebrating the unique ligature-as-key design
**Shaw-JAFL:** Exploratory journey metaphor, emphasizing adventure in learning

All progressions use frequency-based word selection to ensure practical vocabulary from the start.
